Wavelet Transform

  • A wavelet is a special mathematical function for signal processing.
  • It helps to analyze signals that change over time (non-stationary signals).

Why We Use Wavelet Transform?

  • Unlike Fourier Transform (FT), which only gives frequency information, wavelet transform provides both time and frequency details.
  • This makes it perfect for studying sudden changes in signals, like motor vibrations during startup.
